A Friendly Reminder

When playing airsoft, please keep in mind that airsoft is an inherently dangerous sport and without adequate equipment, you may lose an eye or a tooth! So always wear eye and face protection to make sure that you get the most fun and awesome experience while playing airsoft. As always, remember to play in a pleasant manner and always call your hits, even if you're not a hundered percent sure of it! It's just a game so there's no point being mad at people because of simple things. And remember, have fun!
