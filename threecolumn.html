About Panda Force Airsoft

Panda Force Airsoft is a brand new airsoft team which consists of a bunch of friends. Based in Hong Kong, we do various gameplay footage videos, reviews of airsoft guns and accessories, and also some DIY tutorials that you can follow to get the best out of your airsoft guns.

You can check out our airsoft content here, or check out out YouTube channel to find out more about us! Consider subscribing, commenting and liking our videos to recieve up-to-date videos and messages from our channel.

As always, remember to wear adequate eye and face protection, have fun and call your hits! Perhaps you'll run into us when you're airsofting in Hong Kong!

A Friendly Reminder

When playing airsoft, please keep in mind that airsoft is an inherently dangerous sport and without adequate equipment, you may lose an eye or a tooth! So always wear eye and face protection to make sure that you get the most fun and awesome experience while playing airsoft. As always, remember to play in a pleasant manner and always call your hits, even if you're not a hundered percent sure of it! It's just a game so there's no point being mad at people because of simple things. And remember, have fun!
